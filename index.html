Syed Fahad Sultan ( سید فہد سلطان )

I am an Assistant Professor in Computer Science Department at Furman University.

I obtained my PhD in June 2022 from Stony Brook University. During my PhD, I worked in the Data Science Lab and the Lab for Computational Neurodiagostics, advised by Dr. Steven Skiena and Dr. Lilianne Mujica Parodi.

My research interests lie at the intersection of data science, machine learning, computational neuroscience and computational social sciences.
